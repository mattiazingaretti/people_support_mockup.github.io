Project Assumptions

Technical Assumptions
  • SAP Success Factors will remain the core HR platform
  • Current technology infrastructure can support centralized processes
  • API integrations are feasible between systems
  • Data quality is sufficient for analysis
Operational Assumptions
  • Current process documentation is available or can be quickly created
  • Key stakeholders across all countries are accessible
  • Historical data on volumes, FTEs, and costs is available
  • No major organizational restructuring during project
Documentation Requirements
  • Process maps and SOPs (current and historical)
  • Organizational charts and role descriptions
  • Performance metrics and KPIs
  • Technology architecture documentation
  • Compliance and regulatory requirements
Timeline Assumptions

  • Decisions made within 5 business days
  • Weekly progress meetings maintained
