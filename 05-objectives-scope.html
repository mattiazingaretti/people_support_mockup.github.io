Project Objectives

Primary Objective

Support Mercado Libre in evaluating the centralization of HR processes through implementing a Shared Service Center, redefining the Current Operating Model and identifying operational efficiencies and improvements in internal customer experience.

Strategic Goals

  • Gain scalability and efficiency through specialization and standardization
  • Guarantee consistent experience for all employees
  • Strengthen R&D teams and PBP roles
  • Reduce operational costs and risks

Project Scope

1. Organization and Planning (Week 1)

Project setup, team alignment, and planning activities to establish clear objectives and working frameworks.

2. Identification of Opportunities (Weeks 2-3)

  • Understand current process management
  • Assess efficiency and quality of results
  • Evaluate resources, FTEs, and technology
  • Analyze information assets and KPIs
  • Identify pain points and improvement areas
  • Define activities to centralize

3. Business Case & Roadmap (Weeks 4-7)

  • Evaluate costs, benefits, and risks
  • Select best management option
  • Define Operating Model and New Structure
  • Develop Transformation Roadmap
  • Create High-Level Implementation Plan

Out of Scope

  • Detailed implementation execution
  • Technology platform selection and deployment
  • Change management execution
  • Post-implementation support
